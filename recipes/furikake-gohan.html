Transforming Furikake Gohan

Description

A steaming hot bowl of rice topped with eggs and the clothes-busting transforming furikake broth.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Eggs
  • Rice
  • Ginger (grated)
  • Bonito Broth
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Mirin
  • Sake
  • Spring onion (thinly sliced)

Method

  1. Heat sesame oil in frying pan and saut√© chicken wings until golden brown on both sides.
  2. Add chicken wings to pot with broth, ginger, sake, sugar, soy sauce, and mirin. Bring to boil then simmer until reduced by half.
  3. Pour broth into a container to cool. Once cooled, place in refrigerator to chill and harden.
  4. Scramble eggs in pan, whisking hard to create small curds. Set to one side.
  5. Once broth has hardened, cut into small cubes and pour over a bowl of hot rice with the eggs. Garnish with spring onion.
  6. Close the curtains before you taste it.
