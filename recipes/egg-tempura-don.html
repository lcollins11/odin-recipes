Egg Tempura Don

Description

A magically tempura-fried runny egg tops this epic bowl of rice.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Potato Starch
  • Flour
  • Rice
  • Bonito Broth
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sugar
  • Shiso Leaves

Method

  1. Freeze the eggs completely then peel.
  2. Mix together the flour, starch, and some water to make a light batter.
  3. Dust the peeled eggs in flour then coat in the batter. Deep fry eggs, repeat this step for shiso leaves.
  4. Heat broth, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar in a pot and bring to the boil. Simmer for a few minutes to reduce.
  5. Pour a little sauce over the rice then top with the finished eggs and shiso leaves. Pour remaining sauce over the top.
