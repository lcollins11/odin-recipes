Yukihira Style Chaliapin Steak Don

Description

A tenderized sliced steak covered in a mountain of caramalized onion, atop some steaming rice.

Ingredients

  • Steak (flank or sirloin)
  • Onions (finely diced)
  • Rice
  • Umeboshi Paste
  • Spring Onion (finely sliced)
  • Red Wine
  • Soy Sauce
  • Butter
  • Starch (mixed with water)

Method

  1. Score and (if thick) hit steak with a tenderizer, then cover the steak with the diced onion. Rest for 30 mins, then remove the onions and season.
  2. Fry the onion with butter until begins to reach golden brown, season with salt, then set aside.
  3. Fry the steak with more butter in the same pan then remove and slice (against the grain).
  4. Deglaze the pan with red wine and add the onions from earlier, then add soy sauce and the starch, continue to cook.
  5. Pour some sauce over the rice, top with the steak slices then cover it with the onions and remaining sauce. Garnish with some spring onion.
